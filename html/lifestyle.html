Healthy Life style

Dincharya/ Ritucharya:

Ayurveda advocates daily and seasonal regimen to preserve normal health. Dincharya means a daily regimen of life and Ritucharya means a regimen of life in different seasons. If an individual follows a proper daily and seasonal regimen, he may overcome the stress of seasonal variation and may not suffer from any lifestyle disorders.

Ahara- Diet and dietary regimen:

An inappropriate and unbalanced diet is the main reason behind the lifestyle disorders in the body. The ample growth and maintenance of the body mostly depend on an optimum and balanced diet. There is a long list of wholesome diet illustrated in Ayurveda. Ayurveda describes the method of preparation, code, and the discipline of taking a wholesome diet. The selection of the diet of an individual as per bodily constitution is important to maintain optimum health. The diet should be simple, easily digestible, and optimum in quantity. Overeating and consumption of unbalanced diets / tamasa and rajas diets should be avoided because these diets can cause excess overload on the digestion system and metabolic process of the body.

Nidra- Proper rest:

Nidra is the Hindi word for Sleep. It is another essential requirement for optimum health. Good sleep is an important factor for the preservation of health. Today, in a hasty lifestyle, it has been noted that sleep is not given proper attention and consideration that leads to anxiety, depression, stress, and many other lifestyle disorders. An individual needs to take an adequate amount of sleep to avoid lifestyle diseases.

Curbing and controlling of our desires:

It is the third most important component of a healthy life. If an individual balances all its desires such as physical desires, desire to eat fatty foods, desire to take alcohol, tobacco, and drugs, then an individual can maintain a healthy life. One should control or suppress urges of grief, greed, fury, fear, pride, shamelessness, excessive passion, and envy. An individual should practice self-control to avoid being bugged done by negative desires.

Vyayama (Exercise):

It is an important part of a daily regimen.

Moderate physical exercise provides steadiness, lightness, and fortitude in the body. To promote physical and biological strength, it is essential to do regular physical activity daily.
